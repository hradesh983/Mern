About Agriculture Crops

Agriculture crops are plants cultivated for human or animal food, fodder, or commercial products like textiles and spices. Staples like wheat and rice, and products like sugarcane and tea, are grown for sustenance and income. Crop farming is vital for feeding populations, fostering economic growth, and providing employment.
